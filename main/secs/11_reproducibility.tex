\section{Reproducibility Notes}
\label{sec:app_reproducibility}

This section is a good place to collect details needed to reproduce the paper:
data preprocessing, training schedule, evaluation protocol, hardware, random
seeds, and implementation-specific assumptions.

\paperparagraph{Checklist}
Before releasing a project, verify that the following items are documented:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Dataset names, splits, licenses, and preprocessing scripts.
  \item Training hyperparameters, optimizer settings, and hardware budget.
  \item Evaluation commands, metrics, and any excluded or filtered examples.
  \item Checkpoint, code, project page, and dataset links in the title panel.
\end{itemize}
