\section{Broader Impact and Compute Resources}
\label{sec:app_broader_impact}

Appendices are also a natural place to record information that reviewers need
but that would slow down the main narrative: the societal context of the
work and the hardware budget behind the reported numbers.

\parahead{Broader Impact}
Summarize who benefits from the method, who could be negatively affected, and
what safeguards, if any, mitigate that risk.

\parahead{Compute Resources}
Report the hardware used for the main experiments (accelerator type and
count), the approximate wall-clock time per run, and the total compute spent
across the project. This lets readers estimate the cost of reproducing the
paper and compare it against their own budget.
